3D Floor Plans

3D Floor Plans provide 2 main purposes. 1st - a visual blueprint of the entire floor. Whether for personal use or for office space, a 3D Floor Plan is important as it allows a risk-free way to experiment with changes. Changes in 3D Floor Plans is a much cheaper solution than changes after the structure is built
2nd - as a marketing tool. The majority of major construction companies provide a design to show the life a client or clients will live. Real Estate developers have taken a huge liking towards 3D Floor Plans as it allows a buyer an idea of the layout, dimensions, and size.

Interiors

The Interior shot has the main purpose of drawing in the viewer to feel what the project will offer. This is common amongst custom homes as well as any retail fit-outs ( Restaurants, Jewelry, designer stores, etc.)
The Interior shot is very common amongst any project in which people will expect a level of activity or life and in any custom or high-end projects as to make any changes before the structure is created and such changes are irreversible or extremely costly.

Exteriors

The Exterior shot has the main purpose of showing off the house as an attractive asset. Plain and simple people want to have nice things, A mediocre car and a Mercedes will still get you to the same place but one is a positive extension of yourself and the other is a negative display of it.
The Exterior shot also serves for the client to adjust any major concerns with materials, or anything that doesn’t fit right.
